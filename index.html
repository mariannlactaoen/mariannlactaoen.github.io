FAQ!

How long have you been sewing?
I took one (1) summer course in fifth grade and have been self-teaching since!

What machines do you use?
I sew with a Singer 4411 Heavy Duty and serge with a Brother 1034D.

Do you ever make garments for other people or take commissions?
Sorry, no! This artform is very sacred to me and I'd prefer to not monetize it right now.

You'll also note I included a Spotify embed — luckily, Spotify's platform provides
the embed for you, so that was easy and I appreciate the cross-platform guidance.
The most difficult part of this process was inputting and formatting images and video.

altering video controls (autoplay, mute, loop) and inputting image captions.
